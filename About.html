• Fine Dining: Upscale restaurants with an emphasis on high-quality ingredients and skilled chefs.
  • Casual Dining: Relaxed atmosphere, a diverse menu, and typically more affordable than fine dining.
  • Fast Food: Quick-service restaurants known for their convenience and speedy service.
  • Ethnic Cuisine: Restaurants specializing in specific international cuisines, such as Italian, Chinese, Mexican, Indian, etc.
  • Cafés: Often known for coffee and light meals, with a more laid-back atmosphere.
  • Trucks: Mobile kitchens offering a variety of street food and unique dishes.
  • Vegetarian/Vegan Restaurants: Focused on plant-based cuisine.
  • Restaurants: All-you-can-eat options with a range of dishes.
  • Farm-to-Table: Emphasizes using locally sourced, fresh ingredients.
  • Specialty Restaurants: Those that focus on a specific type of food, like sushi, barbecue, or pizza.
